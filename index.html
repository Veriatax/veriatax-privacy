Last updated: 27.01.2026

Veriatax Interactive ("we", "our", or "us") develops and publishes mobile games and applications. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while using our applications.

Our applications may collect the following types of information:

  • Technical information:
    Device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Usage data:
    Basic interaction data such as session duration, gameplay events, or feature usage, used solely to improve app stability and user experience.
  • Advertising data:
    Our applications may display advertisements. Advertising partners may collect anonymous identifiers (such as advertising IDs, IP address, or approximate location) for ad delivery, frequency capping, fraud prevention, and performance measurement in accordance with their own privacy policies.
  • Analytics data:
    Analytics services may collect anonymized usage and performance data to help improve gameplay experience and application quality.

We do not intentionally collect personal information such as names, addresses, or contact details unless explicitly provided by the user (for example, via support contact).

2. How We Use Information

  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Analyze gameplay behavior and usage trends

  • Provide optional in-app features (if applicable)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with trusted third-party service providers that are necessary to operate the application, including advertising networks, analytics providers, and crash-reporting services.

4. Data Retention

Collected data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by applicable laws.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our applications are not specifically directed at children under the age of 13 unless explicitly stated in the app listing. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your data. To make such a request, please contact us using the information below.
